### Line Check
- Definition：Interview coaching PWA that converts resume and job description into speakable answers in English or Chinese, enabling users to practice responses out loud until they feel natural.
- Aliases：对词间、LineCheck

### Greater China
- Definition：Regional designation encompassing mainland China (CN) and Hong Kong (HK) used to determine AI provider selection — triggers DeepSeek instead of Gemini for compliance and latency reasons.
- Aliases：greater-china、greaterchina、cn、hk

### AI Region Header
- Definition：Custom `x-linecheck-ai-region` HTTP header allowing clients to override geo-based AI provider selection, accepting values 'global' or 'greater-china' (with various case/spacing variants accepted).
- Aliases：x-linecheck-ai-region、ai-region

### Fab
- Definition：Floating Action Button — draggable UI element in the interview interface that users can reposition anywhere on screen for convenient access during practice sessions.
- Aliases：floating action button、fab-button

### Q&A
- Definition：Question-and-answer pairs generated from resume and job description, forming the core practice material that users can review, edit, and rehearse aloud.
- Aliases：qa、questions、interview-qa

### Mandatory Questions
- Definition：Pre-defined interview questions that are always included at the top of generated Q&A lists, regardless of resume/JD content, ensuring coverage of essential topics.
- Aliases：mandatory、core-questions

### Focuses
- Definition：Key themes or areas emphasized in interview answers, derived from resume and job description to create targeted practice content aligned with specific role requirements.
- Aliases：focus、focus-areas、themes

### Invent Count
- Definition：Number of additional fictional experience items to generate beyond what's in the resume, allowing candidates to practice discussing plausible but unlisted achievements.
- Aliases：invent、fictional-experience、invented-items

### Practice Set
- Definition：A collection of selected Q&A items grouped together for focused rehearsal, allowing users to work through specific question categories rather than practicing everything at once.
- Aliases：practice-set、selected-qa、rehearsal-set
